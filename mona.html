Mona

AI-Powered Emotional Solutions for New Moms

Category

App UIIUX Design

Role

UI Designer, motion graphic designer

Team

Tianhui Ye, Xinyang Zhang, Zi Lin, Shujie Li, Meng Lan

Award

Indigo Design Award Gold

Overview

Mona is a groundbreaking system designed to support new mothers during the postpartum period, blending wearable technology with AI-driven companionship. Recognizing that 1 in 7 mothers experience postpartum syndrome—often accompanied by stress, isolation, and lack of support—Mona provides a holistic solution to manage both physical and emotional challenges.

Video Demo

Description

The system includes a smart ring for mothers and a baby’s ankle band, both crafted for comfort and safety. These devices monitor vital signs, including heart rate, activity, and emotional indicators for moms and health metrics for babies. Data is seamlessly integrated into an intelligent companion app, offering personalized insights into mood, sleep, and well-being.

Mona’s AI companion provides empathetic support through daily check-ins, mood tracking, and tailored recommendations. It helps mothers navigate emotional shifts and fosters resilience against postpartum depression. A personalized scheduling feature helps moms align their routines with their baby’s patterns, offering actionable suggestions to improve sleep, manage feedings, and balance personal time with caregiving.

Phone-free notifications from the ring ensure mothers can stay connected to important updates while remaining present. Sustainability is prioritized with eco-friendly materials and single-material components that are easy to recycle. Adjustable, durable materials ensure comfort and usability as mothers and infants grow, and wireless dual charging integrates seamlessly into busy routines.

By combining health monitoring with personalized scheduling and empathetic, AI-powered guidance, Mona empowers new moms to manage their well-being and create healthier routines for themselves and their babies, fostering a more nurturing start to family life.
