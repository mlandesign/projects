Service App

Enhancing Efficiency for Truck Drivers

Client

UNIS LLC

Category

UI/UX Design, Mobile App

Role

Requirement Analysis, UI/UX Designer

Timeline

12 Weeks

Overview

The Service App transforms delivery management by digitizing and simplifying operations for UNIS LLC truck drivers. It addresses inefficiencies in traditional processes with features designed to automate tasks, improve communication, and optimize routes, making daily operations seamless.

Project Background

Managing deliveries and shipments has always been a complex and inefficient process for truck drivers at UNIS LLC, relying heavily on manual updates and paperwork. These traditional methods not only slow down operations but also create communication barriers. The Service App was developed to address these challenges by digitalizing essential processes, automating routine tasks, and providing real-time updates. The aim is to improve delivery efficiency, optimize routes, and enhance driver satisfaction, ultimately transforming how drivers interact with their daily tasks.

Value

The Service App revolutionizes delivery management by

1

IMPROVING EFFICIENCY

Real-time updates and automated processes streamline operations, reducing delays

2

REDUCING MANUAL ERRORS

Scanning and reporting features automate routine tasks, minimizing mistakes

3

OPTIMIZING TIME MANAGEMENT

Route guidance and organized job listings help drivers manage deliveries more effectively, saving valuable time and effort

Design Challenges and Solutions

Design System Integration

Challenge

The Business Analyst (BA) provided drafts for My Trip and Open Job that needed to be aligned with our established design system

Solution

Applied our design system guidelines to create cohesive and polished UI components, ensuring visual consistency and user-friendly interactions across both features.

Simplifying Complex UI for Large Parcel

Challenge

The BA's draft proposed multiple features on a single screen, complicating the interface and making it difficult for users to navigate.

Solution

Implemented tabs and segmented controls to organize features efficiently, reducing visual clutter. Leveraged colors and labels to differentiate functions clearly, making the interface intuitive and content easily discoverable.

Reflection & Future Improvements

Launching Service App highlighted the impact of user-centric design in logistics. Future updates may include AI-driven traffic insights for even more optimized routing and expanded features based on continuous driver feedback.

Results

The implementation of the Service App led to a significant 20% increase in delivery efficiency, saving drivers valuable time and reducing operational delays. Manual errors dropped drastically due to automated features like integrated scanning and streamlined reporting, enhancing overall accuracy. Additionally, driver satisfaction scores rose, with positive feedback highlighting the app's intuitive design and easy navigation. The Service App not only improved internal workflows but also set a foundation for future enhancements, such as incorporating real-time traffic data for even greater optimization.
